Schedule in-session wake-ups for yourself: relative one-shots ("in N"), time-of-day one-shots ("at HH:MM" host-local, or an RFC 3339 instant), looping intervals ("every N", re-arming on each fire), and full cron expressions (evaluated in UTC). When a schedule fires, its message is delivered to you as an injected `<agent_message from="norn:cron">` steer — it wakes you if you are lingering or idle, and is queued durably for your next step otherwise. Operations: `schedule` (exactly one of `in`/`at`/`every`/`cron` plus a required `message`), `list` (all pending schedules), `cancel` (by schedule id). Schedules persist as session events and survive a session resume; live timers end with the process.
