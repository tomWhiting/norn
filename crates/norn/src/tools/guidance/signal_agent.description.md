Send a message to another agent: a child you spawned or forked, a sibling, or your parent (the literal "parent"), within the messaging scope you were granted at spawn time. kind "steer" redirects the recipient at its next step boundary and wakes a lingering recipient; kind "update" shares context that batches at step boundaries without waking anyone. Out-of-scope or finished recipients return a structured failure. A valid recipient with no live route returns `queued: true` and is injected when that agent next resumes or wakes.
